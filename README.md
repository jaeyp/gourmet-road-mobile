# gourmet-road-mobile
React-native Gourmet Road client

### main idea
```
Have you ever been disappointed with dishes at famous five star restaurants?
Have you ever tasted treasure-like dishes in a small restaurant that you visited without expecting?

"Gourmet Road" aims to contribute to revitalizing local dining culture and tourism by encouraging locals 
and travelers to experience the most popular dishes of local restaurants without stereotypes and complete 
own “Gourmet Road Maps” and share them with friends.

No stars, No evaluation, "Gourmet Road" gives a fair opportunity to promote the top three dishes of restaurants.
```
